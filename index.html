Biography

I'm a Associate Professor of the School of Rail Transportation at Shandong Jiaotong University. I got my Master degree in the Department of Computer Science and Technology at Kunming University of Science and Technology in 2011, coadvised by Prof. Yuanping Ni.

Research Interests

My research interests include machine learning, artificial intelligence, and data mining. In particular, I am interested in fault diagnosis, bioinformatics, and smart agriculture.
